ESCAPE VELOCITY

After many years on stage and in the studio, Vance is finally releasing his first album under his own name.

Filled with Blues, Rock, Funk, and a little bit of Rockabilly, this album addresses relationships, communication, and a fair amount of space travel.

1. Running Back To You

2. Trouble and Strife

3. Reckless Shenanigans

4. Tell Me What You Want To Do

5. The Things I Did, I Did Not Want To Do

6. Ill-Advised Hijinks

7. Would You Believe Me

8. Snakes In The Garden

9. Hazardous Mischief

10. Baby I Do

11. I Wonder Why

12. Better Than Us

13. Perilous Misbehavior

14. Shouting at the Stars
